Dr. Abdul-Lateef Yussif

Dr. Abdul-Lateef Yussif is a Senior Lecturer and the Head of the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology at the University of Cape Coast. His areas of specialization include Computer Vision and Image Processing, with academic interests in Artificial Intelligence and Image Processing. He earned his Ph.D. in Information Technology from University Teknologi PETRONAS in 2016. Dr. Yussif has contributed to research in his field, with publications such as "The best machine learning mode l for fraud detection on e-platforms: a systematic literature review" and "Preliminary Study on e-Collaboration Readiness and Community of Inquiry Presences in a Higher Educational Institution." He has also been involved in various professional activities, including facilitating workshops and presenting at conferences related to fisheries management and marine conservation.
